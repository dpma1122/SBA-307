Mole (pronounced mo-lay) simply means "sauce" and is a dish native to Mexico. There are many varieties of mole, with the most popular being Mole Rojo and Mole Verde. Traditionally, mole can take hours or even days to prepare, but my recipe is a quick 30-minute version. I'll share my tips and tricks to make it simple and delicious

I was first introduced to mole by my mother-in-law at my sister in law's wedding. Mole is often served on special occasions, and in our family, Mole Rojo is a favorite. It's a perfect balance of spicy and sweet flavors. Its perfect blend of spicy and sweet flavors makes it truly unforgettable.
